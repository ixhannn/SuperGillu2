Privacy Policy

App: Lior (com.lior.app) · Last updated: July 2026

Lior is a private space built for two. This policy explains exactly what we collect, where it's stored, who processes it, and the controls you have. We don't sell your data, show you ads, or track you across the web.

What we collect

We collect only what you give us or what the app needs to run:

  • Account — your email address (for sign-in). If you use Google Sign-In, we receive a Google ID token containing your email and basic profile (name) solely to create your account; we request no other Google data.
  • Profile — your first name and your partner's name, your anniversary and important dates, and app preferences (such as theme).
  • Your content — the photos, videos and voice notes you upload; text notes, open-when letters and captions; dates and countdowns; mood entries; daily-question answers and daily drops; couple-pet interactions; and anything else you create in the app.
  • Notifications — a device push token, so we can alert you when your partner shares something.
  • Diagnostics — if the app hits an error, we log the error message, your app version, and your device's browser user-agent string, linked to your account, so we can fix bugs. We do not log your screen contents, photos, or the text of your memories/notes.
  • Security — to prevent abuse, our sign-in service briefly records the IP address and email address used for login attempts (for rate limiting).

We do not collect your location (the app requests no location permission), your contacts, your browsing history, or any advertising identifier.

Where your data is stored

  • On your device — your content (including cached copies of your photos and videos) and your sign-in session live in the app's private local storage. This stays on your device except when syncing.
  • Supabase (cloud database & authentication) — your account and all your content records, protected by row-level security so only you and your partner can read them.
  • Cloudflare R2 (media storage) — your photos, videos and voice notes, stored as private objects that are only reachable through short-lived signed links (valid ~15 minutes).

How we use your data

Your data is used only to:

  • Display your memories, notes, and moments within the app
  • Sync your content between you and your partner's devices
  • Send notifications when your partner shares something
  • Generate your couple pet's dialogue (see AI features)
  • Keep the service working and secure (bug diagnostics, abuse prevention)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or machine-learning training, and we do not sell or rent it.

AI features (your couple pet)

Your couple pet's dialogue is generated by Google's Gemini API. When the pet speaks, we send Google a small, bounded amount of context to make its reply feel personal:

  • your and your partner's first names
  • how long you've been together and your pet's happiness level
  • the text of your single most recent memory and most recent note (each shortened)

We do not send your photos, videos, voice notes, message history, email, or account identifiers. This context is used only to produce the pet's reply and is handled under Google's API terms. If you'd rather send nothing to Google, simply don't use the pet feature.

Notifications

When your partner does something for you — a heartbeat, answering the daily question, sending a drop — we send a push notification through Google Firebase (or your browser's push service on web). The notification contains your partner's first name and a short template message (for example, "💗 Heartbeat from Alex"), plus which screen to open. It never contains the content of your memories, notes, or answers.

Photos, videos & metadata

When you upload a photo, we re-encode it, which removes embedded metadata such as GPS location and camera details. Videos are currently uploaded without modification and may still contain metadata (including location) that your recording device added — if that concerns you, remove it before uploading. Uploaded media is stored privately and is only viewable through short-lived signed links by you and your partner.

Data retention

  • Daily Moments automatically delete after 24 hours.
  • Everything else you create (memories, keepsakes, time capsules, surprises, voice notes, music) is kept until you delete it, or until you delete your account.
  • Security rate-limit records are short-lived and automatically purged.

You can delete any individual item at any time from within the app.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and data yourself, in the app (Profile → Delete Account). If you are the last active member of your couple, your shared content and all media are deleted too; if your partner is still active, their copy of shared memories is kept while your account and personal data are removed. Deletion is immediate; residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 30 days. Full details on our Account & Data Deletion page.

Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Row-level security (RLS) on every database table — only you and your partner can read your data
  • Media stored privately and served only through short-lived signed URLs
  • Authentication (PKCE) required before any data access; passwords are handled by Supabase Auth and never stored by us in plaintext
  • Rate limiting on sign-in to deter brute-force attempts
  • Device backups of the app are disabled so your session can't be copied to another device

Children's privacy

Lior is intended for adults (18+) or couples. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used this app, contact us and we'll delete their data.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, export, or object to the processing of your data. You can delete your account and content yourself in the app; for anything else, contact us at the email below.

International processing

Our providers (Supabase, Cloudflare, and Google) operate globally, so your data may be processed on servers outside your country. We rely on these providers' standard data-protection commitments.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept it.
