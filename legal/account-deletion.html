Account & Data Deletion

App: Lior (com.lior.app) · Last updated: July 2026

You can permanently delete your Lior account and all of your data at any time — directly inside the app, with no need to email anyone or wait.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Lior and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are erased immediately.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your sign-in account and email
  • Your first name and profile
  • The memories, notes, letters, dates, moods, and moments you created
  • Your uploaded photos and videos (from Cloudflare R2 media storage)
  • Your couple pet and other content you authored
  • Your device push token and your membership in your couple

Deletion is immediate and permanent — it cannot be undone.

If you share a couple with a partner

Lior is built for two people who share one couple space. When you delete your account:

  • If you are the last active member of your couple, the shared content and all media are permanently deleted too.
  • If your partner is still active, their copy of your shared memories is kept for them, while your own account, personal data, and membership are removed.

Timing & backups

Your account and data are removed from our live systems immediately upon confirmation. Any residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 30 days. We retain no personal data beyond what any applicable law requires.

Can't access the app?

If you're unable to open the app to delete your account yourself, email us from the address on your account and we'll process the deletion within 30 days:
